Arriving by Air

Mexico City International Airport (MEX) is your gateway. It's well-connected globally.

From the airport, taxis (authorized airport taxis are safest), Uber, and official airport buses are readily available.

Exploring the City

Mexico City is a treasure trove of culture, art, and gastronomy. Don't miss:

  • The Frida Kahlo Museum (Casa Azul) - a must-visit!
  • Coyoacán neighborhood - vibrant and historic.
  • Chapultepec Park & Castle.
  • Delicious street food and world-class restaurants.
  • A ride on a trajinera in Xochimilco.

For local transport, Uber is widely used and reliable. The Metro system is efficient but can be crowded.
