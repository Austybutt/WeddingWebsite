✈️ Flying into Mexico City

You will fly into Mexico City International Airport. It’s about a 30‑minute Uber to where you’re staying. Several guests are arriving Thursday at 3:17 PM via Dallas.

🚕 Transportation

Uber is the easiest and cheapest way to get around CDMX. Hotels can help with airport pickups, but traffic can be brutal—plan ahead!

🏨 Where to Stay

We recommend Condesa, Roma, Roma Nte., Hipódromo or Juárez—these neighborhoods are safe and walking‑distance to most events. Parque España, Parque México and Plaza Río de Janeiro are great landmarks to search around.

We’ll also have a hotel block near the venue.
